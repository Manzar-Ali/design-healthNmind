Add Over $600k in Additional Net Revenue

RPM and CCM are often interrelated and outcomes are interdependent. As such, CMS allows for reimbursement of both services within the same month. By adding RPM and CPM to your practice, you can realize on average, additional net revenue of over $600,000 per year!

Reduce Readmissions and Improve Outcomes

Virtual Care is proven to improve outcomes and significantly reduce emergent hospital readmissions. With the VitalCare platform, patients can enable clinical remote monitoring of their conditions by using wirelessly connected medical devices to capture their vitals.

Extend Your Patient Reach

Virtual Care allows physicians to treat patients remotely through vitals management, telehealth visits and a robust learning platform. Adding RPM for the collection of daily vitals extends your ability to meet the needs of CCM and PCM patients wherever they are at.

Monitoring Services

Patients who require CCM and RPM will be set up for monitoring. The monitoring service works inside the practice’s EHR. Monitoring functions as an extension of the patient’s care team relieves the burden of adding additional staff to the practice.
